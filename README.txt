CONNECT-4 README (USER GUIDE):

PROGRAM RAN AND CODED ON PYTHON 3.7.2, outdated versions may cause unexpected errors.


Basic instructions of program:

    -Coin flip will decide whether player ('O') or computer ('X') will go first

    -Taking turns, place your piece in the column you think will give you the best shot at connecting 4 of your pieces consecutivly.

    -Game ends when 4 pieces are consecutivly connected either horizontally, vertically or diagonally
